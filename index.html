Destruction of physical files

Once all the material has been scanned correctly, it is no longer necessary to continue paying expensive warehousing to maintain the information. Thus, we also take care of waste paper and massive digital transformation cardboard so you don´t have to worry about it.

Secure storage

It is not enough to digitize the documents; it is necessary that the information transcends the organization and is kept safely. In this way, we complement our services with advice on the safe handling of digital information such as cloud storage.

High speed scanning

Our machines have the ability to scan up to 450 pages per minute (ppm) at 300 dots per inch (dpi), which is one of the fastest machines in the world. This allows us to reduce intervention time, offering faster and more affordable service.

Organization of PDF files

Thanks to our advanced software, post-processing of images is very fast and allows you to create PDF files that represent the contents of a physical folder, enabling precise information retrieval in less time.
