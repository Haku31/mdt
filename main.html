Destruction of physical files 

Once all the material has been scanned correctly, it is no longer necessary to continue paying expensive warehousing to maintain the information. Thus, we also take care of waste paper and massive digital transformation cardboard so you don´t have to worry about it. 

High speed scanning

​our machines have the ability to scan up to 450 pages per minute (ppm) at 300 dots per inch (dpi), which is one of the fastest machines in the world this allow us to reduce the intervention time, offering a faster and more affordable service. 

Organization of PDF files 

Thanks to our advanced software, post-processing of images is very fast and allows you to create PDF files that represent the contents of a physical folder, allowing you to precisely find the information in less time. 

Secure storage 

It is not enough to digitilized the documents; it is necessary that the information transcends the organization and is kept safely. In this way, we complement our services with advice on the safe handling of digital information such as cloud storage.
